About

I oversee the campaigns, own the analytics, and ask the questions most media teams leave to someone else.

I'm Chris Mitchell, a paid media strategist based in Central Ohio, USA with 10+ years of experience turning ad spend into measurable business growth.

My career has been defined by one consistent habit: I build things other people don't bother to build. Custom reporting dashboards. LTV analysis tools. Systems that connect campaign decisions to real business outcomes. I use AI-assisted development to build production-quality analytics tools at a pace that used to require a dedicated engineering team. When the off-the-shelf reporting doesn't tell the full story, I build the tool that does.

Currently I oversee paid acquisition across Google, Meta, TikTok, and more for Breezeline, a national ISP, including the full-funnel launch of a new brand from zero awareness through active customer acquisition. Prior to that, I owned paid media and growth strategy for a DTC supplement brand that scaled to seven figures, and drove acquisition for a SaaS platform with thousands of active users, overseeing everything from campaign architecture and creative testing to funnel optimization and customer retention.

I started my career in education, which shaped how I think: rigorously, from first principles, always asking why something works before deciding whether to scale it.

I'm looking for a remote, in-house role at a growth-stage DTC or subscription brand where I can own performance strategy and stay close to the outcomes.
